About Us

Welcome to the Placement Interaction Tracker System! We are a team dedicated to providing a streamlined platform for managing placements. Whether you're a student seeking career opportunities, an employer looking for top talent, or a placement officer managing the entire process, our system is designed to make the entire experience seamless and efficient.

Our goal is to create a bridge between students and employers, ensuring that the best talent finds the right opportunities, all under the watchful eye of the placement officers who make it possible.
